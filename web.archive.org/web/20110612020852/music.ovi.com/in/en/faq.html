Getting Started

Symphonia Desktop is your go-to music service for enjoying your favorite tracks. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Sign in to your account This is required in order to listen to Music!
  • Search for your favorite songs using Search Bar
  • Click on a Music tile in Homepage or the Play button in the search page to Play Music
  • Compatiblity: All Browsers With Media Session API Chrome 57, Edge 79, Firefox 78, Opera 44
  • Manage your favorites and playlists Coming Soon!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for music?

Use the search bar at the top of the page to find songs by title, artist, or album.

Can I download songs?

Sadly No, You Cannot download Songs Here but can in the Mobile Website!

How do I Register With Symphonia?

From the Symphonia Home Page, Press "Login" button, Press Register and Enter Valid Password

How Do I Listen to Songs?

Click on a Music tile in Homepage or the Play button in the search page to Play Music. To Control Playback, Check the Music Player Button (Usually Near the Search bar). It should show Track Name, Artist and Playback
