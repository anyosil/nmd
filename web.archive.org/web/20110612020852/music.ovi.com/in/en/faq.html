Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an account? +
    To create an account, click on the “Sign Up” button at the top-right corner of the homepage and fill out the registration form with your details.
  • I forgot my password. What should I do? +
    Click on the “Forgot Password?” link on the login page and enter your registered email address to receive a password reset link.
  • How do I reset my profile data? +
    To reset your profile data, go to your account settings, scroll down to “Reset Profile”, and confirm the action. Please note this cannot be undone.
  • Is my data safe and private? +
    Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your data is stored securely and is never shared without your consent.
  • How can I contact support? +
    You can reach out to us anytime via email at Here!. We strive to respond within 24 hours.

Compatibility

Symphonia Desktop works best on modern browsers and devices to provide a smooth and reliable experience. Below are the current compatibility details:

  • Supported Browsers: Chrome (latest), Firefox (latest), Edge (latest), Safari (latest)
  • Mobile: Fully responsive and functional on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and most mobile browsers.
  • Requirements: JavaScript enabled for interaction and dynamic content.
  • Known Issues: Older browsers (IE11 and below) are not supported and may experience display or functionality issues.
